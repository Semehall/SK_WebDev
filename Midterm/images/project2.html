ShakeAwake

Role: Head of Design

Overview: ShakeAwake is a silent, vibration-based alarm designed to help individuals with hearing impairments wake up independently. The device, embedded in a mattress pad, replaces traditional sound-based alarms with customizable vibration patterns.

Problem Statement

Have you ever struggled to wake up on time despite using multiple alarms? Many people, especially those with hearing impairments, face challenges with traditional alarm systems that rely on sound. Standard alarms may not be effective for deep sleepers or individuals with hearing difficulties, leading to missed appointments, late starts, and disrupted daily routines.

To address this issue, we need an innovative solution that ensures a reliable wake-up method without relying on sound alone.

Possible Solution

Introducing ShakeAwake, a smart bed designed to wake users through vibrations rather than sound. ShakeAwake utilizes built-in vibration technology to gently yet effectively wake individuals at their desired time. The bed features customizable intensity levels, ensuring a tailored wake-up experience for different sleepers.

In addition to its vibration mechanism, ShakeAwake can integrate with smart home systems, allowing users to sync their alarms with lighting or mobile notifications.
