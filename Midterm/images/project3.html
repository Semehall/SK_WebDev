ShakeAwake

Role: Product Designer

Overview: ShakeAwake is a silent, vibration-based alarm designed to help individuals with hearing impairments wake up independently. The device, embedded in a mattress pad, replaces traditional sound-based alarms with customizable vibration patterns.

üîπ Problem Statement

Traditional alarm clocks rely on sound, making them ineffective for people with hearing impairments. Many existing vibration-based solutions are uncomfortable or inconvenient.

üîπ My Approach

  • Conducted **user interviews** to understand challenges faced by people with hearing impairments.
  • Developed **low-fidelity prototypes** to test vibration intensity and comfort levels.
  • Created **interactive wireframes** for the mobile app that controls ShakeAwake.
  • Designed a **customizable vibration pattern system** for a personalized wake-up experience.
