ShakeAwake

Role: Head of Design

Overview

ShakeAwake is a silent, vibration-based alarm designed to help individuals with hearing impairments wake up independently. The device, embedded in a mattress pad, replaces traditional sound-based alarms with customizable vibration patterns.

Problem Statement

Many people, especially those with hearing impairments, struggle with sound-based alarms. ShakeAwake aims to offer an inclusive, reliable wake-up method using vibrations instead of sound.

Possible Solution

ShakeAwake uses smart vibration tech with customizable intensity. It can sync with smart home systems to enhance the wake-up experience without stress.

Project Duration

4 Months

Scope of Work & Timeline
