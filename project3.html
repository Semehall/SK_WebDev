ShakeAwake

A Silent, Vibration-Based Smart Alarm Designed for Accessibility

Overview

ShakeAwake is a silent, vibration-based alarm designed to help individuals with hearing impairments wake up independently. The device, embedded in a mattress pad, replaces traditional sound-based alarms with customizable vibration patterns.

Problem Statement

Have you ever struggled to wake up on time despite using multiple alarms? Many people, especially those with hearing impairments, face challenges with traditional alarm systems. ShakeAwake is a smart bed designed to wake users through vibrations instead of sound, ensuring accessibility and independence.

Project Duration

4 months

Scope of Work & Timeline

Our Approach

  • Conducted user interviews to understand hearing-impaired users' needs.
  • Created low-fidelity prototypes and tested vibration levels.
  • Designed a mobile app interface with customizable alarm settings.

Final Outcome

ShakeAwake provides a comfortable, effective, and customizable alarm solution. It empowers users with hearing impairments to wake up independently through smart vibrations and mobile control.
