Vodka Pasta

Total: 30m Prep: 20m Cook: 10m

Instructions

  • Cook pasta, reserving ½ cup pasta water.
  • Sauté onion, garlic, and red pepper flakes in oil and butter.
  • Add tomato paste, cook, then stir in vodka.
  • Mix in cream and Parmesan, season to taste.
  • Toss with pasta, adding pasta water if needed.
  • Serve with Parmesan and basil. Enjoy!

Ingredients

  • 12 oz pasta
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 1 small onion, chopped
  • 3 garlic cloves, minced
  • ½ tsp red pepper flakes
  • ¼ cup vodka
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • ½ cup grated Parmesan
  • Salt & pepper
  • ½ cup pasta water (reserved)
  • Fresh basil (optional)
